Custom YouTube Playlist Shuffler

Create a unique pattern to shuffle your playlists to.

How It Works

You enter the ID of the playlist you want to add in the Playlist Management page. You then use the Shuffler Management page to create a custom pattern to shuffle your added playlists to. You can then go the Playback page, click the Generate Playlist button, and enjoy your newly shuffled playlist.

Why Use This?

You can choose exactly how your playlists mix together, via creating repeatable patterns to use instead of fully random shuffles.
